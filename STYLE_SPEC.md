# TU Dortmund (TuDo) Presentation Style Spec

This spec captures the visual language used by the TU Dortmund University presentation template and the adjustments applied in `presentation.html`.

## Brand Colors

- Primary (TU green): `#83B818`
- Dark green (print/web variant): `#639A00`
- Accent orange (optional, sparing use): `#D98207`
- Black: `#000000`
- Neutral gray (text): `#4A4A4A`
- Light gray (backgrounds): `#F3F5F2`

## Typography

- Sans-serif, clean and modern.
- Headings: bold, TU green.
- Body: regular weight, near-black.
- Emphasis: bold or TU green; avoid excessive italics.

## Layout

- Wide margins with strong left alignment.
- Generous whitespace between blocks.
- Headings are large and prominent; body text is compact and readable.
- Section slides use full-bleed photos with white title text.

## Logos

- TU logo should appear in the footer (left), in full color on white.
- Keep clear space around the logo (no overlapping text or graphics).

## Footer (Fixed)

- Thin TU green rule above the footer area.
- Left: TU Dortmund logo.
- Right: `TU Dortmund University | Presentation` + date + slide number.
- Slide counter format: `current/total` (e.g., `3/22`).
- Footer height ~58 px on 1280x720.

## Lists and Bullets

- Use TU green bullets/markers.
- Avoid nested bullets; keep lists short and scannable.

## Tables and Charts

- Clean, minimal grids.
- TU green for key highlights and headings.
- Neutral grays for secondary information.

## Imagery

- Bright, natural lighting.
- People and campus life preferred.
- Avoid dark or heavy overlays; keep text contrast high.

## Do / Don’t

- Do use TU green consistently for headings and accents.
- Do keep layouts clean with clear information hierarchy.
- Don’t overcrowd slides; prioritize clarity.
- Don’t introduce new colors unless necessary and brand-approved.
